# Paris Lights - Fiche concept V2
**Provider:** French Gambling  
**Game Name:** Paris Lights  
**Format:** Video slot, 5 reels x 3 rows  
**Lines:** 20 fixed lines  
**UI Language:** English  
**Theme:** Elegant Paris by night, iconic landmarks and luxury symbols  
**Wild:** Golden Paris Emblem  
**Scatter:** Illuminated Eiffel Tower  
**Bonus:** Free Spins with expanding global multiplier via Street Lanterns
## Pitch du jeu
A stylish Parisian slot set in a glowing night-time city of elegance, luxury and iconic landmarks.  
Each spin celebrates the most recognizable symbols of Paris, from grand monuments to refined objects, in a premium and readable slot experience. During Free Spins, Street Lanterns build a growing global multiplier, turning the city lights into a rising win feature.

## Mécanique bonus
### Free Spins Feature
- **3 Scatters** = **10 Free Spins**
- **4 Scatters** = **12 Free Spins**
- **5 Scatters** = **15 Free Spins**
### Bonus rule
- During Free Spins, each **Street Lantern** adds **+1 to the global multiplier**
- The bonus starts at **x1**
- Each Lantern increases the multiplier permanently for the rest of the feature
- All wins during Free Spins are paid with the current global multiplier
**Idée clé pour la V2 :**  
The bonus should feel like the city lights are intensifying with every Lantern collected, building momentum and value in a simple and satisfying way.
## Ton visuel
- Elegant, premium, cinematic
